Total Accidents due to Bad Weather Conditions

Adverse weather conditions such as heavy rain, thick fog and hail storms make driving riskier as visibility reduces and road surface gets slippery. The data of road accidents shows that almost 3/4th of the accidents took place under sunny/clear weather. The data showed that bright clear days witnessed 73.3% of India’s total road crashes in 2017. Contrary to popular perception, rainy days saw just 5.83% of India’s road crashes. Foggy and misty days witnessed 5.45% around accidents. Cloudy conditions claimed 5.18% of total lives lost. As per the report, more than 70 per cent of fatal road crashes involved adults in the age group of 18-45.
